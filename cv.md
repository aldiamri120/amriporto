## Projects

### FinStream App Redesign (FinTech)
Reimagining the stock investment experience for a millennial audience.

* **Problem**: Users felt overwhelmed by complex financial data, leading to a 35% drop-off rate on the transaction screen.
* **Solution**: Streamlined the information hierarchy and introduced a tiered 'Easy Mode' for novice investors.
* **Results**:
    * Boosted transaction conversions by 20%.
    * Winner of 'Best UI' at the 2025 Design Awards.
    * Elevated Net Promoter Score (NPS) from 6 to 9.

### ZenHealth Wellness Tracker (Health Tech)
A comprehensive mental health and meditation tracking application.

* **Problem**: Competitor apps felt clinical and failed to incentivize daily user engagement.
* **Solution**: Integrated gamification elements and a calming palette rooted in color psychology.
* **Results**:
    * Increased Daily Active Users (DAU) by 40%.
    * Extended average meditation session duration by 15 minutes.
    * Achieved a 2.5x growth in user engagement rates.

### Nexus Design System (Design Ops)
Establishing a singular 'Source of Truth' for a global product team.

* **Problem**: Widespread design inconsistencies across Web, iOS, and Android platforms.
* **Solution**: Developed a comprehensive Atomic Design component library in Figma with exhaustive documentation.
* **Results**:
    * Accelerated design-to-dev handovers by 30%.
    * Eliminated 90% of visual UI inconsistencies.
    * Doubled overall design process efficiency.
